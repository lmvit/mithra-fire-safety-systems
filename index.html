Alarm System

Wet Pipe System

Water in the system's pipes ready to be activated by the opening of fire sprinkler heads due to heat

Dry Pipe System

This system has air or nitrogen gas in the pipes and water is released into the pipes upon activation of the sprinkler heads after a slight delay.

Deluge

Activated by smoke or heat detectors, deluge equipment releases a dousing flood of water or form in an instant through an open-sprinkler, nozzle, or foam generator

Pre-Action

This is a combination of wet and dry pipe fire sprinkler systems and are not activated until a fire is detected
